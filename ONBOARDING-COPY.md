## ✦ COPY NOTES FOR DESIGN

- **Screens 1–2:** Full-bleed obsidian background. kool red on the headline. White subtext.
- **Screen 3:** Feature cards on dark cards with kool red icon accents. Not a bulleted list — cards breathe.
- **Screen 4:** Light input fields on obsidian. CTA button in kool red. Feel like a launch pad, not a form.
- **Screen 5:** Premium screen should feel earned, not pushy. Use the founding member badge as warmth, not urgency bait. The skip link must be easy to see — trust = conversion long term.

---

## ✦ VOICE REMINDERS

- never capitalize brand name in running copy: always `kool`, `kool events`, `kool♥`
- the app doesn't "help" or "allow" — it *does*. active verbs only.
- no exclamation points. confidence doesn't shout.
- no "we're so excited" — paula isn't. she's serious about the work.
